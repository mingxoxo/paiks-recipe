Tofu Kimchi


#spicy #soft


[Ingredients]


1 cup kimchi (200g)
1/2 cup water (90g)
1/4 onions (40g)
1/5 cup carrot (20g)
1/4 cup green onion (20g)
1 cup pork(160g)
1 hat of tofu (300g)
1 chungyang red pepper (14g)
1 red pepper (20g)
1/2 tablespoon of brown sugar (5g)
1 tablespoon of liver (15g)
1.5 tablespoons thick red pepper (15g)
2 tablespoons sesame oil (15g)
1/2 tablespoon sesame seedling (3g)
3 tablespoons of soy sauce (30g)

[Recipe]


1. Spread a sheet of pork belly in a frying pan and sprinkle with yellow sugar to brown.
2. When samgyeopsal is cooked, add water and boil it with thick red pepper, garlic, and soy sauce.
3. Add kimchi and stir-fry altogether when the seasoning is mixed.
4. Add onions and carrots and stir-fry slightly. Add green peppers, red peppers, and green onions and turn off the heat.
5. Mix evenly and mix with sesame oil.
6. Put sesame oil in boiling water and blanch tofu in a bowl.
7. Put tofu in a dish and cut it well to eat.
8. Put stir-fried kimchi on one side and sprinkle sesame salt to finish.
