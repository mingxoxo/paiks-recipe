Fried Beef over Rice
(Fried Beef-deopbap)


#sweet #salty


[Ingredients]


A bowl of Rice
Chopped Green Onion
Frying Flour
Cooking Oil
1 spoon Worcester Sauce
3 spoon Soy Sauce
1.5 spoon Sugar
3 spoon Vinegar
3 spoon Cooking Wine
A little bit of Ginger powder

[Recipe]


1. Cut the beef into appropriate sizes and remove the blood. Sprinkle the beef with salt and ground pepper.
2. To make the sauce, mix worcester sauce, soy sauce, sugar, vinegar, cooking wine, and a little bit of ginger powder.
3. Put frying powder on the beef.
4. Heat the pan with plenty of oil, and fry the powdered beef.
* Be careful not to burn, because it is so thin.
5. Place the crispy fried beef on a kitchen towel.
6. Put a bowl of rice in the bowl and place the cooked beef on it.
7. Spread the sauce with the right amount.
