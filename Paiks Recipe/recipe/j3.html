Black Bean Sauce Noodles
(Jajangmyeon)


#spicy #solty #soft


[Ingredients]


Raw Kalguksu Noodles
300g of Chunjang
Green Onion
Cabbage
Cucumber
Green young pumpkin
3 cups of chopped Onions
2 cups of grounded Pork
3 spoons of grounded Ginger
2/3 cup sugar
1/3 cup soy sauce
1 cup cooking oil
1 spoon of starch

[Recipe]


1. Add plenty of cooking oil, add Chunjang and fry it on medium heat for about 10 minutes. When it's done, Cool it down,
* Please be careful not to burn.
2. Cut the vegetables (Green Onion, Cucumber, Onion, Green young pumpkin) into appropriate sizes and trim them.
3. Pour cooking oil in the pan and put chopped Green Onion, then fry it to make green onion oil.
4. Before the green onion is done, put the pork in and fry them together.
5. By the pork is done cooking, pour the soy sauce in.
6. Put the vegetables in.
7. When vegetables cooked, put the sugar and fried Chunjang in.
8. Then, pour a bowl of water in, and mix them well with Jajang.
9. When it boils, put the starch in and control the concentration.
10. Finally, cover them with a slight of cooking oil to give them a gloss.
11. Shake the starch off the noodles and rinse them under running water.
12. Put the noodles in the boiling water and stir them.
13. Add a little bit of cold water so that the noodles don't get lumpy each time it boils.
14. Repeat them about 2 times to cook the noodles to the core.
15. Plate the noodles in the bowl and put the sauce on it.
